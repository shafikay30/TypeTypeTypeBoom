About TamilTutor

easyTutor is an interactive online platform that aims to connect students, lecturers and alumni all in one place to provide a more sourceful and engaging learning experience.

At easyTutor, students will be able to access all related notes and materials for any class and can share their own understanding or review for each class.Students will also be able to discuss a topic with other students, lecturers and alumni through the chatroom and discussion corner.
